## Wiring:
- Temperature : Red - 5V , Black - GND , blue - 9 + resistance between red and blue
- Movement : GND - GND , VCC - 5V , SCL - SCL , SCA - SCA
- SD card :  3V3 on 3.3V, CS on pin 10, MOSI on pin 11, CLK on pin 13, MISO on pin 12, GND on GND
